What is Aviation?

Aviation is the science and practice of flight through the air. It encompasses everything from the design and operation of aircraft to the infrastructure that supports air travel. For me, aviation represents the perfect marriage of human ingenuity and technological progress.

Pilot License Path

PPL

Private Pilot License

  • Fly single-engine aircraft
  • Cannot be paid to fly
  • ~50-70 flight hours
  • Basic navigation & meteorology

CPL

Commercial Pilot License

  • Fly for compensation
  • ~150-200 flight hours
  • Advanced flight operations
  • Instrument rating required

ATPL

Air Transport Pilot License

  • Command aircraft >5700kg
  • ~1500 flight hours
  • Airline transport operations
  • Highest civilian certification

My planned path: Complete Computer Science degree → Save for flight training → Earn PPL → CPL → ATPL while working in aviation-tech field

Why Africa Needs Tech-Savvy Pilots

Africa's aviation industry is projected to grow by 5% annually, faster than the global average. Yet the continent faces:

  • Shortage of trained aviation professionals
  • Limited adoption of modern aviation technologies
  • Insufficient infrastructure for growing demand
  • Need for localized solutions to unique challenges

As a Ghanaian with both technical and aviation training, I aim to bridge this gap by:

  • Developing cost-effective training tools
  • Creating systems tailored to African aviation needs
  • Mentoring the next generation of African aviators
  • Advocating for technology adoption in regional aviation
