Aviation & Flight Training

George’s journey to the skies begins here.

What is Aviation?

Aviation is the science and practice of flight. It includes everything from piloting small aircraft to managing complex airline systems. It’s where physics, engineering, and passion meet the skies.

Types of Licenses

  • PPL: Private Pilot License — for non-commercial flying
  • CPL: Commercial Pilot License — to earn as a pilot
  • ATPL: Airline Transport Pilot License — top-tier license to fly for airlines

George’s Flight Plan

After completing his Computer Science degree focused on AI, George aims to enroll in an accredited pilot training school in Canada or Ghana, earn his PPL, then CPL, and eventually become ATPL-certified.

Tools Pilots Use

  • Flight Management Systems (FMS)
  • Glass Cockpits & HUDs
  • Flight Planning Software
  • Autopilot & Avionics Systems

Why Africa Needs Tech-Driven Pilots

Africa’s aviation sector is growing fast. But we lack pilots who understand both the cockpit and the code. George’s mission is to fill that gap — building tools, simulations, and systems for safe, smart African skies.
